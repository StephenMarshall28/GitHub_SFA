🌺 Hibiscus

Hibiscus is a tropical flowering plant known for its large, colorful blooms. It is commonly grown in gardens and hedges and is also the national flower of Malaysia. Hibiscus plants are attractive, easy to maintain, and bloom frequently in warm climates.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight (6–8 hours daily) for best flowering.

💧 Watering

Water regularly to keep soil moist but not waterlogged.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 24°C – 32°C. Prefers warm tropical climates.

🪴 Soil

Use fertile, well-draining soil rich in organic matter.

✂️ Blooming

Flowers bloom frequently throughout the year in warm conditions.

🌸 Uses

Used for decoration, landscaping, hedges, and ornamental gardens

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Prepare a sunny garden area with well-draining soil.
  2. Plant hibiscus seedlings or cuttings into soil.
  3. Water thoroughly after planting.
  4. Place in full sunlight for best flowering.
  5. Space plants properly for growth.
  6. Apply fertilizer regularly.
  7. Prune to encourage more blooms.

❤️ Benefits

  • Beautiful ornamental flowering plant
  • National flower of Malaysia
  • Attracts bees and butterflies
  • Blooms frequently in warm climates
  • Easy to grow and maintain
  • Used for landscaping and decoration

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Yellow Leaves

Cause: Overwatering or poor nutrients.
Solution: Adjust watering and apply fertilizer.

2. Bud Drop

Cause: Stress from temperature or watering changes.
Solution: Maintain stable care conditions.

3. Leaf Spots

Cause: Fungal infection.
Solution: Remove affected leaves and improve airflow.

4. Pests (Aphids / Whiteflies)

Cause: Insect infestation.
Solution: Use natural pest control methods.

5. Slow Flowering

Cause: Lack of sunlight or nutrients.
Solution: Provide full sunlight and fertilizer.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Provide full sunlight daily
  • Water regularly but avoid waterlogging
  • Use fertilizer for better blooms
  • Prune regularly for shape and flowering
  • Remove dead flowers to encourage new blooms
  • Protect from strong winds if needed

🌸 What You Can Do

  • Use for garden decoration
  • Create hedges or borders
  • Decorate homes and parks
  • Use for landscaping projects
  • Attract pollinators to gardens
  • Sell as ornamental plants

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Hibiscus rosa-sinensis
  • Family: Malvaceae
  • National flower of Malaysia (Bunga Raya)
  • Blooms year-round in tropical climates
  • Common ornamental plant worldwide
  • Symbol of beauty and unity
