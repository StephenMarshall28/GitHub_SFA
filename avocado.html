🥑 Avocado

Avocado is a tropical fruit known for its creamy texture and high nutritional value. It grows on medium to large trees and can be planted in home gardens with enough space. Avocado is popular for healthy diets and modern recipes.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight (6–8 hours daily) for healthy growth.

💧 Watering

Water moderately. Keep soil moist but avoid waterlogging.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 20°C – 30°C. Prefers warm conditions.

🪴 Soil

Use well-draining soil rich in organic matter.

✂️ Harvest

Harvest when fruits are mature. They soften after picking.

🍝 Uses

Used in salads, smoothies, spreads, and healthy meals

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Prepare a planting area with fertile, well-draining soil.
  2. Plant avocado seedlings or grafted plants.
  3. Water thoroughly after planting.
  4. Place in an area with full sunlight.
  5. Ensure enough space for the tree to grow.
  6. Maintain regular watering and care.
  7. Fruiting may take several years.

❤️ Benefits

  • Rich in healthy fats and nutrients
  • Supports heart health
  • High in vitamins and antioxidants
  • Popular in healthy diets
  • Good for skin and overall health
  • Valuable fruit for home growing

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Root Rot

Cause: Overwatering or poor drainage.
Solution: Improve drainage and reduce watering.

2. Leaf Browning

Cause: Salt buildup or water stress.
Solution: Flush soil and adjust watering.

3. Fruit Drop

Cause: Stress or lack of nutrients.
Solution: Ensure proper fertilization and care.

4. Insects (Mites / Aphids)

Cause: Pest infestation.
Solution: Use natural pest control methods.

5. Slow Growth

Cause: Poor soil or lack of sunlight.
Solution: Improve soil and provide full sunlight.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Water moderately and avoid overwatering
  • Ensure good drainage
  • Apply fertilizer regularly
  • Provide full sunlight exposure
  • Prune for better shape and airflow
  • Monitor for pests and diseases

🍃 What You Can Do

  • Use in salads and healthy meals
  • Make smoothies and spreads (e.g., guacamole)
  • Grow in home gardens
  • Sell avocados locally
  • Start a small fruit business
  • Include in daily diet

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Persea americana
  • Family: Lauraceae
  • Originated from Central America
  • Popular worldwide for its health benefits
  • Requires several years to produce fruit
  • Thrives in warm climates like Malaysia
