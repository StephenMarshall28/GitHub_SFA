🌸 Bougainvillea

Bougainvillea is a vibrant climbing ornamental plant known for its bright, papery flowers in pink, purple, red, or white. It is commonly used to decorate fences, walls, and gardens, and thrives well in tropical climates like Malaysia.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight (6–8 hours daily) for best flowering and color.

💧 Watering

Water moderately. Allow soil to dry slightly between watering.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 24°C – 35°C. Loves hot and sunny weather.

🪴 Soil

Use well-draining, slightly dry soil. Avoid overly rich soil for better blooming.

✂️ Blooming

Blooms multiple times a year under proper sunlight and pruning.

🌸 Uses

Used for fences, walls, landscaping, garden decoration, and hedges

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Prepare a sunny area with well-draining soil.
  2. Plant stem cuttings or seedlings into soil.
  3. Water lightly after planting.
  4. Provide support like fences or trellises for climbing.
  5. Place in full sunlight for best flowering.
  6. Allow soil to dry slightly between watering.
  7. Prune regularly to shape growth.

❤️ Benefits

  • Bright and colorful ornamental plant
  • Perfect for garden decoration
  • Fast-growing climbing plant
  • Creates natural privacy screens
  • Low maintenance once established
  • Blooms frequently in tropical climates

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Leaf Drop

Cause: Overwatering or sudden weather changes.
Solution: Reduce watering and stabilize environment.

2. Few Flowers

Cause: Too much shade or fertilizer.
Solution: Provide full sunlight and reduce fertilizer.

3. Root Rot

Cause: Poor drainage or overwatering.
Solution: Improve drainage and reduce watering.

4. Pests (Aphids / Caterpillars)

Cause: Insect infestation.
Solution: Use natural pest control methods.

5. Overgrowth

Cause: Fast-growing vine nature.
Solution: Regular pruning and trimming.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Provide full sunlight for best blooms
  • Do not overwater
  • Prune regularly to control shape
  • Use poor to average soil for better flowering
  • Train vines on fences or walls
  • Remove dead branches and flowers

🌸 What You Can Do

  • Decorate fences and walls
  • Create colorful garden landscapes
  • Use for hedges and privacy screens
  • Enhance home garden design
  • Grow in outdoor spaces
  • Use in landscaping projects

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Bougainvillea spp.
  • Family: Nyctaginaceae
  • Native to South America
  • Thorny climbing ornamental plant
  • Very drought-tolerant once established
  • Popular in tropical and subtropical gardens
