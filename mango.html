🥭 Mango

Mango is a tropical fruit known for its sweet, juicy taste and rich flavor. It grows on large trees and is widely cultivated in warm climates like Malaysia. Mango trees can produce many fruits and are popular in home gardens and farms.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight (6–8 hours daily) for healthy growth and fruit production.

💧 Watering

Water regularly when young. Mature trees need less frequent watering.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 24°C – 32°C. Thrives in warm climates.

🪴 Soil

Use well-draining soil rich in organic matter.

✂️ Harvest

Harvest when fruits are mature and slightly soft with full color.

🍝 Uses

Used for fresh eating, juice, desserts, and cooking

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Select a large planting area with fertile soil.
  2. Plant mango seedlings or grafted plants.
  3. Water thoroughly after planting.
  4. Ensure full sunlight exposure.
  5. Provide enough space for tree growth.
  6. Maintain regular care and fertilization.
  7. Fruiting may take several years.

❤️ Benefits

  • Rich in vitamins A, C, and antioxidants
  • Supports immune system
  • Popular and widely consumed fruit
  • High economic value
  • Suitable for home gardens and farms
  • Provides shade and greenery

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Powdery Mildew

Cause: Fungal infection in humid conditions.
Solution: Improve airflow and apply treatment.

2. Fruit Fly Damage

Cause: Insects attacking fruits.
Solution: Use protective covering and pest control.

3. Leaf Spot

Cause: Fungal disease.
Solution: Remove affected leaves and improve hygiene.

4. Flower Drop

Cause: Weather stress or lack of nutrients.
Solution: Maintain proper care and fertilization.

5. Slow Growth

Cause: Poor soil or lack of sunlight.
Solution: Improve soil and provide full sunlight.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Provide full sunlight exposure
  • Water regularly when young
  • Apply fertilizer for better fruiting
  • Prune branches for better airflow
  • Monitor for pests and diseases
  • Ensure proper spacing

🍃 What You Can Do

  • Eat fresh mango or make juice
  • Use in desserts and cooking
  • Grow in home gardens or farms
  • Sell mangoes locally
  • Start a small fruit business
  • Use in daily meals

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Mangifera indica
  • Family: Anacardiaceae
  • Known as the “King of Fruits” in some regions
  • Long lifespan tree
  • Thrives in tropical climates like Malaysia
  • Comes in many varieties
