🌼 Golden Trumpet

Golden Trumpet is a fast-growing ornamental flowering plant known for its bright yellow trumpet-shaped flowers. It is commonly used in landscaping, roadside planting, and gardens due to its vibrant color and attractive appearance. It thrives well in tropical climates like Malaysia.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight (6–8 hours daily) for best flowering and growth.

💧 Watering

Water regularly but allow soil to dry slightly between watering.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 22°C – 32°C. Prefers warm tropical climates.

🪴 Soil

Use well-draining soil rich in organic matter.

✂️ Blooming

Blooms multiple times a year under proper sunlight and care.

🌸 Uses

Used for landscaping, roadside decoration, gardens, and ornamental planting

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Prepare a sunny area with well-draining soil.
  2. Plant seedlings or stem cuttings into soil.
  3. Water thoroughly after planting.
  4. Ensure full sunlight exposure.
  5. Provide enough space for spreading growth.
  6. Apply fertilizer for better flowering.
  7. Prune occasionally to maintain shape.

❤️ Benefits

  • Bright yellow ornamental flowers
  • Excellent for landscaping projects
  • Fast-growing and low maintenance
  • Improves garden and roadside beauty
  • Attracts pollinators like bees
  • Suitable for tropical climates

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Root Rot

Cause: Overwatering or poor drainage.
Solution: Improve soil drainage and reduce watering.

2. Leaf Yellowing

Cause: Nutrient deficiency.
Solution: Apply balanced fertilizer regularly.

3. Weak Flowering

Cause: Insufficient sunlight.
Solution: Provide full sun exposure.

4. Pests (Aphids / Caterpillars)

Cause: Insect infestation.
Solution: Use natural pest control methods.

5. Overgrowth

Cause: Fast-growing nature.
Solution: Regular pruning and trimming.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Provide full sunlight daily
  • Water moderately and avoid waterlogging
  • Use fertilizer for better blooms
  • Prune regularly to control growth
  • Remove dry or damaged branches
  • Ensure good spacing between plants

🌸 What You Can Do

  • Use for landscaping and gardens
  • Decorate roadsides and parks
  • Create colorful flower borders
  • Enhance outdoor scenery
  • Use in public garden projects
  • Grow as ornamental plant

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Allamanda cathartica
  • Family: Apocynaceae
  • Also known as Golden Trumpet Vine
  • Native to tropical regions
  • Produces bright yellow trumpet-shaped flowers
  • Common in Southeast Asian landscaping
