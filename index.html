Welcome to Smart Farming Assistant!

Smart Farming Assistant is a platform built to support local farmers and food growers across Malaysia. We provide accessible knowledge, practical tools, and real connections to help you grow better and sell smarter.

From learning how to plant and care for crops to connecting with suppliers and buyers, everything you need is gathered in one place. It is designed to improve productivity and promote sustainable farming.

🌱 Learn farming the right way
🤝 Connect with local communities
🛒 Sell directly, no middlemen
📈 Grow smarter, earn better

What you can do here

🔎 Explore Plants

Discover different types of plants, along with their benefits and practical uses.

📘 Learn to Grow

Follow step-by-step guidance on how to plant and grow crops successfully in your environment.

💧 Daily Care

🛡️ Pest & Disease Help

👩🏻‍🌾 Who are we?

We are students from Universiti Teknologi Malaysia who developed Smart Farming Assistant as part of our academic project. Our goal is simple, to create something useful for the community.

This platform is designed for anyone interested in growing food, whether you're a beginner starting a small garden or someone managing a larger farm. We aim to make knowledge more accessible while helping users connect, trade, and grow together.
