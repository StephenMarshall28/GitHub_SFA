Welcome to Smart Farming Assistant!

This is a platform designed to empower and to equip local farmers and food growers across Malaysia with accessible knowledge and practical support. This website serves as an interactive information hub where users can explore detailed guides on various crops, including how to plant, maintain, and protect them from common diseases. Beyond learning, this platform also allows you to look at our database to find other local farmers, gardening equipment sellers, fertilizer sellers, and even find other retailers, helping you'all to discover potential markets and sell your produce without relying on middlemen. By combining education and direct market access, this Smart Farming Assistant website aims to support sustainable farming practices, improve productivity, and to assist the local agricultural community.

What you can do here

On this platform, you can explore a wide range of resources to support your farming journey from start to harvest. Learn about different types of plants, including their benefits and uses, and discover step-by-step guidance on how to plant and grow them successfully in your own environment. You can also find practical tips on daily care, such as watering, fertilizing, and maintaining healthy soil conditions. If your plants face issues, this Smart Farming Assistant website provides helpful information on identifying common diseases and pests, along with solutions to treat and prevent them. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced grower, this platform is designed to guide you in making better farming decisions and achieving healthier, more productive crops.

Who are we?

We are a group of university students from Universiti Teknologi Malaysia, who developed the Smart Farming Assistant as part of our academic project with the goal of creating something meaningful for the community. Our mission is to provide a helpful platform where individuals can learn about food growing, whether for small-scale home gardening or medium-scale farming activities. Through this website, users can gain valuable farming knowledge, explore crop information, and connect with others through our available database for trading and selling produce. We believe this project can benefit anyone who is interested in growing their own food, improving self-sustainability, and building stronger local farming networks.
