🌹 Rose

Rose is one of the most famous ornamental flowering plants in the world, known for its beautiful petals and pleasant fragrance. It is widely grown in gardens, parks, and decorative landscapes. Roses come in many colors and symbolize love, beauty, and respect.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight (6–8 hours daily) for best flowering and healthy growth.

💧 Watering

Water regularly, keeping soil moist but not waterlogged. Avoid wetting leaves.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 18°C – 28°C. Prefers mild and cool climates.

🪴 Soil

Use fertile, well-draining soil rich in organic matter.

✂️ Blooming

Blooms several times a year with proper pruning and care.

🌸 Uses

Used for decoration, bouquets, gardens, perfumes, and gifting

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Prepare a sunny garden area with well-draining soil.
  2. Plant rose cuttings or seedlings into soil.
  3. Water thoroughly after planting.
  4. Ensure full sunlight exposure.
  5. Space plants properly for airflow.
  6. Apply fertilizer regularly for better blooms.
  7. Prune to encourage healthy flowering.

❤️ Benefits

  • Beautiful and highly decorative flower
  • Strong fragrance for gardens and homes
  • Symbol of love and beauty
  • Used in perfumes and decorations
  • Available in many colors and varieties
  • Improves garden aesthetics

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Black Spot Disease

Cause: Fungal infection in humid conditions.
Solution: Remove infected leaves and improve airflow.

2. Powdery Mildew

Cause: Fungal growth on leaves.
Solution: Use proper spacing and reduce humidity.

3. Aphids

Cause: Sap-sucking insects.
Solution: Use natural insect control or soap spray.

4. Leaf Drop

Cause: Stress from watering or environment changes.
Solution: Maintain consistent care routine.

5. Poor Flowering

Cause: Lack of sunlight or nutrients.
Solution: Provide full sunlight and fertilizer.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Provide full sunlight daily
  • Water at soil level, avoid wetting leaves
  • Prune regularly for better blooms
  • Use fertilizer during growing season
  • Remove dead flowers (deadheading)
  • Ensure good airflow around plants

🌸 What You Can Do

  • Use for bouquets and gifting
  • Decorate gardens and homes
  • Grow for landscaping projects
  • Use in perfumes and fragrances
  • Sell flowers commercially
  • Create romantic garden spaces

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Family: Rosaceae
  • One of the most popular flowers worldwide

  • Widely used in culture and traditions
