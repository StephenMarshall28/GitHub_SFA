🍉 Watermelon

Watermelon is a refreshing fruit known for its high water content and sweet taste. It grows as a vine plant and thrives in warm climates. Watermelon is suitable for home gardens with enough space and is popular for its cooling effect.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight (6–8 hours daily) for healthy growth and fruit development.

💧 Watering

Water regularly, especially during fruit development. Avoid overwatering.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 22°C – 32°C. Prefers warm conditions.

🪴 Soil

Use sandy, well-draining soil rich in organic matter.

✂️ Harvest

Harvest when the underside turns yellow and fruit sounds hollow when tapped.

🍝 Uses

Used for fresh eating, juices, desserts, and refreshing drinks

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Prepare a large area with well-draining soil.
  2. Sow seeds about 2–3 cm deep.
  3. Water gently and keep soil moist.
  4. Place in a sunny area with full sunlight.
  5. Seeds usually germinate within 5–10 days.
  6. Allow vines to spread or provide space.
  7. Fruits develop within a few months.

❤️ Benefits

  • High water content for hydration
  • Rich in vitamins A and C
  • Refreshing and low in calories
  • Easy to grow in warm climates
  • Produces large fruits
  • Suitable for home gardening

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Powdery Mildew

Cause: Fungal disease in humid conditions.
Solution: Improve airflow and avoid wet leaves.

2. Root Rot

Cause: Overwatering or poor drainage.
Solution: Improve drainage and reduce watering.

3. Fruit Cracking

Cause: Irregular watering.
Solution: Maintain consistent watering.

4. Pests (Aphids / Beetles)

Cause: Insects feeding on plant.
Solution: Use natural pest control methods.

5. Small Fruits

Cause: Poor soil or lack of nutrients.
Solution: Improve soil and fertilize regularly.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Provide full sunlight exposure
  • Water consistently
  • Ensure good drainage
  • Allow space for vines to spread
  • Use fertilizer for better fruit growth
  • Remove damaged leaves

🍃 What You Can Do

  • Eat fresh watermelon
  • Make juices and refreshing drinks
  • Grow in home gardens
  • Sell fruits locally
  • Start a small fruit garden
  • Use in desserts

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Citrullus lanatus
  • Family: Cucurbitaceae
  • Vine plant that spreads across the ground
  • High water content fruit
  • Thrives in warm climates like Malaysia
  • Harvest time depends on variety
