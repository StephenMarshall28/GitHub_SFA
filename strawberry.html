🍓 Strawberry

Strawberry is a sweet and juicy fruit loved for its bright red color and refreshing taste. It is commonly used in desserts, drinks, jams, cakes, and healthy snacks. Strawberries grow well in cool environments and are suitable for pots, hanging baskets, gardens, and small farming projects.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs 6–8 hours of sunlight daily for healthy fruit production and sweetness.

💧 Watering

Water regularly to keep soil moist but avoid soaking the roots or fruits.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 15°C – 26°C. Cooler climates help produce sweeter fruits.

🪴 Soil

Use fertile, well-draining soil rich in organic matter and compost.

✂️ Harvest

Fruits can be harvested when fully red and ripe, usually 2–3 months after planting.

🍰 Uses

Used in desserts, smoothies, cakes, jams, ice cream, juices, and healthy snacks.

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Prepare fertile and well-draining soil in pots or garden beds.
  2. Plant strawberry seedlings with enough space between each plant.
  3. Water gently after planting to keep soil moist.
  4. Place in sunny area with proper airflow.
  5. Remove weeds and dry leaves regularly.
  6. Harvest strawberries once fruits become fully red.

❤️ Benefits

  • Rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants
  • Supports healthy immune system
  • Sweet healthy snack option
  • Beautiful fruit for home gardens
  • Can be grown in pots or hanging baskets
  • Suitable for small farming and business projects

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Root Rot

Cause: Overwatering and poor drainage.
Solution: Improve drainage and reduce excessive watering.

2. Mold on Fruits

Cause: High humidity and poor airflow.
Solution: Space plants properly and remove rotten fruits.

3. Yellow Leaves

Cause: Nutrient deficiency or overwatering.
Solution: Add compost and water carefully.

4. Insects (Aphids / Slugs)

Cause: Common garden pests attacking leaves and fruits.
Solution: Use natural pest control and keep garden clean.

5. Small Fruits

Cause: Lack of sunlight or nutrients.
Solution: Increase sunlight exposure and fertilize properly.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Water soil consistently without flooding
  • Remove damaged or rotten fruits quickly
  • Add compost regularly for better fruit growth
  • Keep plants in sunny and cool environment
  • Protect fruits from insects and birds
  • Trim runners if focusing on fruit production

🍃 What You Can Do

  • Grow strawberries at home in pots
  • Sell fresh strawberries locally
  • Make strawberry jam or desserts
  • Use in cakes and drinks
  • Start small fruit farming projects
  • Decorate gardens with strawberry plants

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Fragaria × ananassa
  • Family: Rosaceae family
  • Popular fruit grown worldwide
  • Can grow in containers and hanging baskets
  • Flowers are usually white with yellow centers
  • Fresh strawberries are highly perishable and should be eaten quickly
