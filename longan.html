🟤 Longan

Longan is a tropical fruit tree known for its small round fruits with sweet translucent flesh. It is commonly grown in Southeast Asia and thrives in warm tropical climates. Longan fruits are popular for fresh eating, desserts, and drinks, making them suitable for orchards, home gardens, and tropical farming projects.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight daily for healthy tree growth and better fruit production.

💧 Watering

Water regularly to maintain moist soil, especially during dry weather.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 22°C – 34°C in warm and humid tropical environments.

🪴 Soil

Use fertile, well-draining soil rich in compost and organic nutrients.

✂️ Harvest

Fruits are harvested when they become light brown and fully ripe.

🍹 Uses

Consumed fresh, used in desserts, herbal drinks, canned fruits, and sweet soups.

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Prepare fertile soil with good drainage and compost.
  2. Plant longan seedlings in open sunny area with enough spacing.
  3. Water regularly after planting to maintain soil moisture.
  4. Apply fertilizer regularly for healthy growth.
  5. Remove weeds and prune damaged branches when necessary.
  6. Harvest fruits once they become fully brown and sweet.

❤️ Benefits

  • Rich in vitamins and antioxidants
  • Sweet refreshing tropical fruit
  • Provides shade in gardens and orchards
  • Suitable for tropical fruit farming
  • Popular fruit in local markets and desserts
  • Can be enjoyed fresh or dried

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Leaf Yellowing

Cause: Nutrient deficiency or poor watering.
Solution: Add fertilizer and water consistently.

2. Root Rot

Cause: Poor drainage and excessive watering.
Solution: Improve soil drainage and avoid overwatering.

3. Fruit Drop

Cause: Water stress or lack of nutrients.
Solution: Maintain regular watering and fertilization.

4. Insects (Fruit Borers / Ants)

Cause: Common tropical fruit pests.
Solution: Keep orchard clean and inspect fruits regularly.

5. Slow Growth

Cause: Poor soil quality or insufficient sunlight.
Solution: Improve soil fertility and provide full sunlight.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Water consistently during dry seasons
  • Apply compost and fertilizer regularly
  • Prune weak or damaged branches
  • Keep planting area clean from weeds
  • Monitor fruits for pests and diseases
  • Provide full sunlight for better fruiting

🍃 What You Can Do

  • Grow longan trees in orchards or home gardens
  • Sell fresh longan fruits locally
  • Make desserts and herbal drinks
  • Use as tropical shade tree
  • Start small fruit farming projects
  • Share fruits with family and neighbors

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Dimocarpus longan
  • Family: Sapindaceae family
  • Related to rambutan and lychee fruits
  • Popular tropical fruit in Southeast Asia
  • Can be eaten fresh or dried
  • Known for sweet translucent flesh and shiny black seed
