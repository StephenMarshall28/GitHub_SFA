🌸 Vanda Orchid

Vanda Orchid is a beautiful tropical orchid known for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers. It is commonly grown as an ornamental plant in gardens, hanging baskets, and greenhouses. This orchid is highly valued for its striking colors and elegant appearance.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs bright, indirect sunlight or partial sunlight for healthy blooming.

💧 Watering

Water regularly but allow roots to dry slightly between watering. Do not overwater.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 20°C – 32°C. Prefers warm and humid tropical conditions.

🪴 Soil

Grown without soil in orchid baskets or mounts with good air circulation.

✂️ Blooming

Blooms several times a year under proper care, with long-lasting flowers.

🌸 Uses

Used for decoration, floral arrangements, and ornamental gardens

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Place orchid in a hanging basket or mount (no soil needed).
  2. Ensure roots are exposed to air and light.
  3. Water by soaking roots 2–3 times per week.
  4. Place in bright, filtered sunlight.
  5. Maintain good air circulation around the plant.
  6. Apply orchid fertilizer occasionally.
  7. Wait for flowering once plant matures.

❤️ Benefits

  • Beautiful ornamental flowering plant
  • Long-lasting and vibrant blooms
  • Enhances garden and home aesthetics
  • Suitable for hanging decoration
  • Valuable in floral arrangements
  • Popular in tropical gardening

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Root Rot

Cause: Overwatering or poor air circulation.
Solution: Allow roots to dry between watering and improve airflow.

2. Leaf Yellowing

Cause: Too much direct sunlight or nutrient deficiency.
Solution: Move to filtered light and fertilize properly.

3. Flower Drop

Cause: Stress from temperature or watering changes.
Solution: Maintain stable environment and care routine.

4. Pest (Mealybugs / Aphids)

Cause: Insect infestation.
Solution: Clean plant and use mild insect control.

5. Dry Roots

Cause: Lack of humidity or water.
Solution: Increase humidity and soak roots regularly.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Provide bright but indirect sunlight
  • Do not use regular soil
  • Maintain high humidity
  • Water by soaking roots
  • Use orchid fertilizer occasionally
  • Ensure good air circulation

🌸 What You Can Do

  • Use for home and garden decoration
  • Create floral arrangements
  • Grow in hanging baskets
  • Decorate balconies or patios
  • Use in wedding and event decoration
  • Sell as ornamental plant

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Vanda spp.
  • Family: Orchidaceae
  • Epiphytic orchid (grows on air, not soil)
  • Common in Southeast Asia
  • Highly valued ornamental flower
  • Can bloom multiple times a year
