🍍 Pineapple

Pineapple is a tropical fruit plant known for its sweet and juicy flavor. It is easy to grow and requires minimal maintenance, making it suitable for home gardens and small farming. Pineapple plants are compact and can even be grown in pots.

☀️ Sunlight

Needs full sunlight (6–8 hours daily) for best growth and fruit development.

💧 Watering

Water moderately. Pineapple is drought-tolerant but prefers slightly moist soil.

🌡️ Temperature

Best growth at 22°C – 32°C. Thrives in warm climates.

🪴 Soil

Use sandy, well-draining soil to prevent waterlogging.

✂️ Harvest

Harvest when fruit turns golden yellow and gives a sweet aroma.

🍝 Uses

Used for fresh eating, juice, desserts, and cooking

🌱 How To Plant

  1. Cut the crown (top) from a fresh pineapple.
  2. Remove lower leaves and allow it to dry for 1–2 days.
  3. Plant the crown into well-draining soil.
  4. Water lightly and keep soil slightly moist.
  5. Place in a sunny area with full sunlight.
  6. Roots develop within a few weeks.
  7. Fruiting may take several months to over a year.

❤️ Benefits

  • Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants
  • Supports digestion
  • Easy to grow and maintain
  • Can be grown in small spaces
  • Produces sweet and nutritious fruit
  • Suitable for home gardening

⚠️ Common Diseases & Problems

1. Root Rot

Cause: Overwatering or poor drainage.
Solution: Use well-draining soil and reduce watering.

2. Leaf Browning

Cause: Too much sun or water stress.
Solution: Adjust watering and provide balanced sunlight.

3. Slow Growth

Cause: Lack of nutrients.
Solution: Add fertilizer regularly.

4. Pests (Mealybugs / Mites)

Cause: Insect infestation.
Solution: Use natural pest control methods.

5. Small Fruit

Cause: Poor soil or insufficient care.
Solution: Improve soil and provide proper nutrients.

🌿 Care Tips

  • Provide full sunlight exposure
  • Water moderately and avoid overwatering
  • Use well-draining soil
  • Apply fertilizer occasionally
  • Remove dead leaves
  • Be patient as fruiting takes time

🍃 What You Can Do

  • Eat fresh pineapple or make juice
  • Use in desserts and cooking
  • Grow in pots or home gardens
  • Sell pineapples locally
  • Start a small fruit garden
  • Use for healthy meals

ℹ️ Extra Info

  • Scientific Name: Ananas comosus
  • Family: Bromeliaceae
  • Can be grown from the fruit crown
  • Each plant produces one fruit
  • Thrives in tropical climates like Malaysia
  • Low-maintenance plant
